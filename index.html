What is Tap It?

Tap It! creates custom 3D printed keychains with built-in NFC technology. When someone taps the keychain with their smartphone, it instantly opens a website, contact card, or interactive experience.

No apps required — just tap and it works.

What Can Our NFC Tags Do?

  • Open a digital business card
  • Share social media instantly
  • Display a daily fun fact
  • Show a Bible verse
  • Promote your business or website
  • Create unique interactive experiences

How It Works

1. Hold your smartphone near the NFC keychain.
2. Your phone automatically opens the programmed page.
3. Instantly access the content.

It's that simple.
